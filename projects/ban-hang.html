01 Project Overview

Thái Sơn Mobile E-Commerce Website is an e-commerce project built on WordPress with the WooCommerce plugin, developed for the E-Commerce course at Nam Can Tho University.

The website specializes in providing technology & electronic products such as phones, laptops, tablets, and accessories. The project was carried out by a team of 3 students and deployed on a live hosting environment.
