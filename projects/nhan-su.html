01 Project Overview

The Human Resource Management System (HRMS) is a comprehensive software solution that manages all HR-related processes in an enterprise — including recruitment, onboarding, employee records, time management (leave, timesheet), performance evaluation, asset management, document management, and offboarding.

The project was carried out as part of the graduation internship at Phúc Lâm Phương Software Technology Co., Ltd. (PLPSoft) under the supervision of Mr. Võ Văn Phúc — the company's Director.

02 Problems & Goals

Real-world Problems at PLPSoft

  • Lack of data centralization: Employee records, contracts, evaluations, leave requests... were stored across Excel, Word, and paper — difficult to search and control.
  • Non-standardized processes: No clear process for onboarding, offboarding, or leave approval — leading to errors and delays.
  • Legal compliance risks: Employment contracts and leave policies not aligned with Vietnam's Labor Law — potential disputes.
  • Performance evaluation challenges: Lack of tools for periodic reviews, skill tracking, and training management.

Project Goals

  • Data centralization: 100% of HR data stored in a single, unified system.
  • Process automation: Reduce leave request processing time from days to under 2 hours.
  • Enhanced security: Password encryption, RBAC, JWT token authentication.
  • Performance assurance: Dashboard loads under 2 seconds, API responses under 500ms.
  • Scalability: Support at least 100 concurrent users.

04 System Architecture

The system is designed using Microservices architecture with 7 independent modules:

Auth_ServiceJWT login, RBAC (3 roles)
Employee_ServiceProfile, timesheet, KPI, departments, org charts

Equipment&Document_ServiceAsset management, digital signature documents
