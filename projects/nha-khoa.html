01 Project Overview

The Dental Clinic Management System is a software solution that digitizes the entire operations of a dental clinic — including patient management, intelligent appointment scheduling (with automatic conflict detection), electronic health records, services and pricing, staff and payroll, multi-payment billing, inventory management, and statistical reporting.

The project was undertaken with the goal of minimizing management errors, optimizing patient experience, and enhancing operational efficiency for the clinic.

02 Problems & Goals

Real-world Problems

  • Distributed data: Patient records, appointments, and medical histories were manually managed on paper — easily lost and difficult to retrieve.
  • Complex scheduling: Manual appointment booking frequently led to overlapping schedules and conflicts between doctors.
  • Billing errors: Manual treatment cost calculation and payment processing often resulted in mistakes.
  • Delayed reporting: Revenue and performance reports were slow, inaccurate, and not timely for decision-making.

Project Goals

  • Comprehensive digitalization: Transition from paper-based to centralized electronic system.
  • Intelligent scheduling: Automatically detect scheduling conflicts using 7 constraint conditions.
  • Electronic health records: Secure storage, fast retrieval, and high data protection.
  • Real-time reporting: Enable timely data-driven decision making.

05 Appointment Conflict Detection

The system automatically validates 7 conditions when booking an appointment:

1 Selected shift
2 Valid check-in time
3 Patient not double-booked
4 Doctor not overloaded
5 Chair not broken
6 Chair not full
7 Valid service

Note: Each doctor can accept a maximum of 10 patients per day and 3 patients per hour.
